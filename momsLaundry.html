ABOUT US!

WHAT IS EVERGLO?

Welcome to Everglo, where laundry isn't just a chore – it's a family affair! Founded in 2019 by the passionate and dedicated CEO, Amaka Onwuama. We understand that in the fast-paced world we live in, laundry can be a daunting task. That's why, at Everglo, we've made it our mission to take the load off your shoulders – quite literally! Our team combines youthful energy with years of experience, ensuring that your clothes are treated with care and attention to detail. Whether you're a busy professional or a seasoned individual that needs reliable service, Everglo is here to make your laundry experience seamless and stress-free. Join our Everglo family, where we treat your laundry as if it were our own!

WHY CHOOSE US?

Choosing Everglo isn't just selecting a laundry service; it's embracing a personalized and hassle-free experience that sets us apart from the rest. What makes us your top choice? Imagine your laundry being handled with the precision of a family heirloom – each garment, a cherished member receiving individualized attention. At Everglo, we blend the energy of youth with the wisdom of experience, creating a dynamic team that treats your clothes like they're walking the red carpet. We're not just here to wash; we're here to pamper your garments, ensuring they leave feeling rejuvenated and ready for their next adventure. Forget the mundane and opt for the extraordinary – choose Everglo, where laundry isn't just a service, but a celebration of fabric, family, and freshness! Your clothes deserve the VIP treatment, and we're here to deliver it with a touch of Everglo magic.
